Die Hard
Die Hard can be described as rough, exciting, and clever.
The plot revolves around a one-man army, a master criminal, and a tough law enforcer.
The main genres are action and thriller.
In terms of style, Die Hard has a Hollywood tone.
In approach, it is serious and realistic.
It is set, at least in part, in a skyscraper.
Die Hard is located in Los Angeles.
It takes place in the 1980s.
It is based on a book.
Die Hard has received attention for being a modern classic, essential viewing, and a blockbuster.
It is well suited for a boys' night.
Note that it involves profanity and violent content.
