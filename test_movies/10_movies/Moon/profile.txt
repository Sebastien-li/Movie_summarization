Moon
Moon can be described as bleak, contemplative, and atmospheric.
The plot revolves around isolation, space and aliens, and imprisonment or confinement.
The main genres are independent, sci-fi, and drama.
In terms of style, Moon is futuristic and is surreal.
In approach, it is serious.
The storytelling is slow paced.
Moon is set, at least in part, on the moon and in outer space.
The movie has received attention for being critically acclaimed and an award winner.
