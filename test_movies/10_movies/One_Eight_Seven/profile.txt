187
187 can be described as disturbing, gloomy, and rough.
The plot revolves around hardcase students, juvenile delinquency, and teachers and students.
The main genre is drama.
In approach, 187 is serious and realistic.
It is set, at least in part, at a high school and in an urban environment.
It is located in Los Angeles.
187 takes place in the 1990s.
Note that it involves brief nudity, drugs/alcohol, and profanity.
